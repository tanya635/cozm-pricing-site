Fee FAQs

What is included in the per-filing fee?

Each filing includes full tax assessment, documentation preparation, submission to the relevant authority, and correspondence handling until resolved. You also get real-time status tracking and proactive deadline reminders through our client portal.

Is there a minimum filing volume or spend commitment?

No. You are only charged for filings actually submitted. If you have zero filings in a given month, there is no charge. There is no minimum volume requirement.

How does the volume discount work in practice?

Discounts are calculated on a rolling 12-month basis. Once you cross a tier threshold, the higher discount applies from that point forward. Tiers only move upward — you never lose a discount mid-year.

Which countries do you cover?

We currently support automated compliance filings across 45+ countries covering Europe, North America, Asia-Pacific, and the Middle East. Our coverage is expanding quarterly based on client demand.

How is quality maintained at lower prices?

Our team includes former Big 4 professionals with 10–20 years of experience. Lower pricing reflects technology-driven efficiency. Every filing is reviewed by a qualified professional before submission.

Can you integrate with our existing HRIS?

Yes. We offer pre-built integrations with 25+ platforms including SAP SuccessFactors, Workday, Oracle HCM, and Concur. Custom API integrations are also available.

What if a filing is rejected by an authority?

No additional charge. If a filing is rejected due to an error on our part, we resubmit at no cost. If rejection is due to incorrect data, we work with you to correct and resubmit — also at no charge.

What happens to our existing Big 4 relationship?

Many clients transition gradually. The Cozm can run alongside your current provider during a parallel period, allowing you to benchmark accuracy and cost before fully switching. Zero disruption to ongoing compliance.

How is data security handled?

All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We are fully GDPR compliant and ISO 27001 certified, with regular third-party security audits. Enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure.

Do the fees increase each year?

Fees are fixed for each 12-month contract. Any adjustments are communicated at least 90 days before renewal and are capped at the lower of 3% or the Eurozone CPI. Per-filing fees have never increased since launch.
